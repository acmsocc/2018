The conference is located in Cape Rey Beach Resort in Carlsbad, California. This page provides travel information and other information about nearby attractions and venues.

Travel Information

The following major airports are all candidates for flying in.

  • San Diego International Airport (SAN) (30 miles)
    SAN is the closest international airport to the conference venue and it t is within a 30 miles distance. SuperShuttle and other shuttle services operate between SAN and the hotel and can be booked in advance. An Uber ride is estimated at $50 one way but is subject to traffic conditions and time.
  • Los Angeles International Airport (LAX) (100 miles)
    LAX is the largest accessible international airport. There is a higher chance of finding a more convenient flight to/from LAX (i.e., direct flights, cheaper, and more options). However, it is a little bit far away and there is a chance that you will hit the heavy traffic of Los Angeles on your way. Being busy all the time, it might not give you the best travel experience. It was ranked second-to-last airport in the US in 2017 in a study by J.D. Power.
  • John Wayne Airport (JWA) (57 miles)
    While not as big as LAX, JWA ranked best airport in the same study that put LAX second-to-last. It is also closer than LAX. You might not find a lot of flight options, but you might be able to skip (most of) the heavy traffic of Southern California.
  • Long Beach Airport (LGB) (75 miles)
    LGB is another airport in Southern California which will allow you to escape some of the heavy traffic. It is a little bit further away than John Wayne but you can still consider it a viable option.
  • Ontario International Airport (ONT) (95 miles)
    ONT is almost as far as LAX in distance but is not usually very busy and the traffic around it is better than Los Angeles.

Nearby Hotels

Cape Rey Carlsbad is a very nice and convenient hotel to stay in. We negotiated a special rate of $199/night for the attendees but you might still want to explore other options.
  • Holiday Inn Express (16 minutes walk)
  • Ramada by Wyndham (20 minutes walk)
  • La Quinta Inn & Suites (20 minutes walk)
  • Omnia La Costa Resort & Spa (15 minutes drive): This is where OSDI 2018 will be located.

Shopping and Dining

Poinsettia Village is a nearby shopping center with many cuisine options. It is just within a walking distance from the hotel and it has a pharmacy, a shipping center, two banks, and many restaurants with several cuisines including American sub, burger, pizza, sushi, Chinese, Mexican, and others.

Nearby Attractions

Souther California is one of the nicest places for a nice vacation and activities. Here are a few attractions around the hotel.
  • The beach (Across the street)

    Right across from the hotel, there is a publicly accessible and very nice beach that you can enjoy. The beach is accessible from 7:00 AM to sunset. Be ready to take a long stair-case on your way to the beach.

  • LEGOLAND (<5 miles)
    The famous LEGOLAND Park is only 5 miles away from the conference. You can meet Emmet, Wyldstyle, Lego Batman, and your other favorite characters.
  • SeaWorld (30 miles)
    SeaWorld is only 30 miles away and is right outside San Diego so you do not have to worry about major traffic on your way.
  • San Diego Zoo (30 miles)
    With 100 acres and more than 650 species, San Diego Zoo is one of the largest zoos in the entire world. San Diego Zoo is located in only 30 miles away and you can spend the entire day exploring the different species over there.
  • Disneyland Park (70 miles)
    Established in 1955, Disneyland Park is one of the earliest theme parks in the world. Its icon Cinderella Castle, is one of the most photographed structures in the United States. Disneyland is closer to Los Angeles but is worth the trip.
  • Universal Studios (104 miles)
    A trivia question: What do Kung Fu Panda (Master Po), Shrek, and the Minions have in common? Answer: They are all at Universal Studios. In 2017, Universal Studios attracted more than nine million visitors which puts it ahead of the Eiffel Tower in Paris.
  • Hollywood Walk of Fame (102 miles)
    Do you want to find out if Donald Trump's star at Hollywood Walk of Fame is still there? You can go there and check out yourself. You can also find your favorite star among 2,600 stars installed in the sidewalk. It is a little bit far away from the conference venue so you would better combine it with other activities such as visiting The Getty.
  • The Getty (106 miles)
    Art, architecture, and gardens, all in one place. Admission is always free but you still have to pay for parking if you decide to drive.
